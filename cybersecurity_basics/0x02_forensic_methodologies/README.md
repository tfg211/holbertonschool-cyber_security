# Cybersecurity Basics: Forensic Methodologies

Welcome to the world of Digital Forensics! This project introduces the core concepts, ethics, and methodologies used in digital forensic investigations. It involves analyzing digital evidence, understanding the legal and ethical implications, and utilizing standard forensic tools to uncover hidden information.

## 📖 Learning Objectives
By the end of this project, you should be able to explain the following concepts without the help of Google:

* **Fundamentals:** What digital forensics is and its importance in modern investigations.
* **Ethics & Integrity:** The critical role of ethics, objectivity, and data integrity during an analysis.
* **Methodologies & Standards:**
    * The ACPO principles for computer forensics.
    * The standard stages of the digital forensic process.
    * Guidelines set by organizations like SWGDE, DFRWS, and NIST.
* **Evidence Handling:**
    * Ensuring evidence is admissible in court.
    * The definition and crucial importance of the **Chain of Custody**.
    * Proper preservation of digital evidence.
* **Practical Skills:**
    * Documenting findings in a professional forensic report.
    * Using common tools (like `exiftool`) to extract metadata and uncover anomalies.

## 🛠 Requirements
* **Operating System:** Linux (Kali, ParrotOS, or Ubuntu 22.04+).
* **Editors Allowed:** `vi`, `vim`, `emacs`.
* All analyses and metadata extractions are performed in a controlled environment to simulate real-world digital investigations.
